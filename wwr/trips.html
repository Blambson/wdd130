Alex leads our solo groups. He is super chill. He will guide you on a lovely tour down the river. You will enjoy 3 hours of rafting, doing your best to keep up with Alex as he leads the way.

Ben is a dreamer. He leads our two person adventures. You will team up with a stranger and make a new friend. Close bonds form as you are left to fend for yourself in a realistic survival situation. You will practice rafting, fishing, building a shelter, and general survival skills until Ben remembers to come and get you. This advenure is not for the faint of heart.

Cathy is a skilled rafting guide, perfect to lead groups of 3 or more on a white water rafting adventure of a lifetime. You will be in groups of 6 in your rafts, and up to three rafts can go together at a time. Cathy knows CPR, basic feild medicine, and also how to yodel. Your life will never be the same!
